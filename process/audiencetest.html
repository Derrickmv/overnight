Audience Test

Tasks

  1. Figure out how to move to different areas/scene of the game.
  2. Learn one thing about art from the game.
  3. Make it to the end of the game.

Evaluation

  1. How often do you play browser games?
  2. How often do you play PC games?
  3. Did you read the instructions before starting the game?
  4. Did you skip the video in the beginning? If yes, why?
  5. How clear was the navigation? (i.e. the arrows, clickable items, etc.)
  6. Were you able to keep up with the dialogue? Did it go by too quickly? Too slowly?
  7. How was the wording for the educational bits? Too boring? Just right?
  8. How would you rate the story? 1 being extremely boring. 5 being extremely interesting.
  9. In the past 6 months, have you played a game similar to this? If yes, which ones?
  10. Do you feel like the theme fit the information being displayed?
